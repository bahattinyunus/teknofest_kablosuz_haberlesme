# Contributing to Wireless-Architect

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the **Wireless-Architect** project! We welcome contributions from fellow tech enthusiasts and IT architects in the field of wireless communication.

## How to Contribute

1.  **Fork the Repository:** Create your own copy of the project.
2.  **Create a Branch:** `git checkout -b feature/your-feature-name`
3.  **Make Changes:** Implement your enhancements or technical documentation.
4.  **Commit Changes:** `git commit -m 'Add some feature'`
5.  **Push to Branch:** `git push origin feature/your-feature-name`
6.  **Open a Pull Request:** Describe your changes and why they are valuable.

## Code of Conduct

*   Be respectful and professional.
*   Focus on technical excellence and clear documentation.
*   Share knowledge openly to foster growth in the TEKNOFEST community.

Happy Coding!
